Can I really get a home-based FFL License?

YES! My copyrighted process has a 150% money back guarantee and has been used successfully by thousands of customers in all 50 states.

Premises [your home] for conducting business you certify that:

  • business to be conducted under the license is not prohibited by state or local law in the place where the licensed premise is located;
  • within 30 days after the application is approved business will comply with the requirements of state and local law applicable to the conduct of the business;
  • the business will not be conducted under the license until the requirements of state and local law applicable to the business have been met; and,
  • the applicant has sent or delivered a form to the chief law enforcement officer where the premises are located notifying the officer that the applicant intends to apply for a license.
  • secure gun storage [Gun Safe NOT required] or safety devices will be available at any place in which firearms are sold under the license to persons who are not licensees ("secure gun storage or safety" defined in 18 U.S.C. 921 (a) (34) ).
